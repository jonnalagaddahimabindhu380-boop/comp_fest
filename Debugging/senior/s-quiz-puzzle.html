Senior Advanced Logic Puzzles

Time: 20:00

1. Five people sit in a row. Each person wears a different color shirt and plays a different sport. Use the clues to determine the arrangement. Alice sits next to Bob. Carol doesn't play tennis. Dave plays basketball. Eve doesn't wear red. Who sits in the middle?

2. A bridge can support a maximum weight of 200kg. Four people weigh 50kg, 75kg, 80kg, and 95kg respectively. Only two people can cross at a time, and at least one torch-bearer must accompany them. If the torch takes 5kg, what is the minimum number of crossings needed?

3. There are 100 prisoners and 100 boxes, each numbered 1-100. Each box contains a random prisoner's number. All prisoners must open exactly 50 boxes to succeed. What is the optimal strategy to maximize their probability of success?

4. A merchant wants to transport wine across three cities. He has 8 identical bottles of wine, but one contains poison. He has one test subject. What is the minimum number of tastings needed to identify the poisoned bottle?

5. You have a 5-minute hourglass and an 8-minute hourglass. How can you measure exactly 7 minutes?
